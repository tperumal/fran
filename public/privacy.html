FRAN — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 4, 2026

Overview

FRAN is a personal life management app. We collect only what's necessary to provide the service — your email address and the data you enter (tasks, meals, workouts, etc.).

Data We Collect

Email address — used for authentication (sign in/sign up). Processed by Supabase Auth.

User-generated content — tasks, meal plans, workouts, bills, and other data you enter. Stored in Supabase (hosted on AWS) and accessible only to you and your household members.

Location data — used only for the weather widget. Processed locally in your browser via the Open-Meteo API. We do not store your location.

Voice input — voice commands are transcribed locally using your browser's speech recognition. The transcribed text is sent to our server to determine intent. We do not store voice recordings or transcripts.

Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking of any kind. We do not sell or share your data with third parties. We do not use cookies for tracking.

Household Sharing

If you create or join a household, certain data (tasks, meals, grocery lists, bills, weekend plans) is shared with other household members. This is an opt-in feature you control.

Data Storage

Your data is stored in Supabase (PostgreSQL) with row-level security. Each user can only access their own data and data shared within their household.

Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.
